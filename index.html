QUESTIONS

FAQ

How long does a bag take to make?

Most pieces take between 5–14 days to complete, depending on the design and current order volume. Custom orders may take longer.

Do you ship outside Nigeria?

Yes — Madebysuxy ships worldwide. International shipping cost and timeline are confirmed with you directly on WhatsApp after checkout.

How do I pay?

Add your pieces to cart and checkout — this sends your order straight to WhatsApp, where Suzy will confirm your total and payment details.

Can I request a custom colour?

Absolutely. Use the "Customize Yours" button to send your preferred colours and reference photos.
